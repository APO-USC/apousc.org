President's Welcome


Hello and welcome! My name is Sharyse and I am the current President of Alpha Phi Omega at USC. Through our cardinal principles of Leadership, Friendship, and Service, we aim to make an impact on both the local community and the lives of our members. Our vision is to be recognized as the premier service-based leadership development organization. We invite you to join us in our mission to develop leadership, promote friendship, and provide service to humanity!

In LFS,

Sharyse Watanabe | President Fall 2019
